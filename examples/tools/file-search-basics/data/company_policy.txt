ACME CORPORATION COMPANY POLICIES

VACATION POLICY
Employees are entitled to 15 days of paid vacation per year. Vacation time accrues at a rate of 1.25 days per month. Employees must request vacation at least 2 weeks in advance. Unused vacation time may be carried over to the next year, up to a maximum of 5 days.

REMOTE WORK POLICY
Employees may work remotely up to 2 days per week with manager approval. Remote work days must be scheduled in advance and employees must be available during core business hours (9 AM - 3 PM EST). All remote work must be performed in a professional environment.

EXPENSE REIMBURSEMENT
Business expenses over $25 require pre-approval. Receipts must be submitted within 30 days of the expense. Approved expenses will be reimbursed within 2 weeks of submission. Mileage is reimbursed at the current IRS rate.

PROFESSIONAL DEVELOPMENT
The company provides $2,000 per year per employee for professional development activities including conferences, training courses, and certifications. Employees must obtain manager approval before registering for any professional development activities.

CODE OF CONDUCT
All employees are expected to maintain professional behavior and treat colleagues with respect. Harassment, discrimination, and unprofessional conduct will not be tolerated. Any violations should be reported to HR immediately.
